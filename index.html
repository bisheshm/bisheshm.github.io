Soccer For Nepal

Soccer For Nepal is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that I created during my sophomore year of high school. My organization collects used soccer equipment (mainly cleats), and donates them to children in Nepal. Nepal is one of the poorest countries in the world, and many children play soccer barefoot in harsh conditions. As a Nepalese soccer player myself, I wanted to help these children and contribute to my birth country of Nepal.
www.soccerfornepal.org
